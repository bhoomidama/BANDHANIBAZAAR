The Art of Bandhni

Bandhni, also known as Bandhej, is a traditional tie-dye art form primarily practiced in Rajasthan and Gujarat, India. The word 'Bandhni' is derived from the Sanskrit word 'Banda', which means 'to tie'.

This intricate process involves tying small knots on the fabric with thread, which resist the dye, creating beautiful patterns like dots, squares, waves, and stripes. Each Bandhni saree is a testament to the artisan's skill and dedication.

At Bandhni Bliss, we bring you authentic, handcrafted Bandhni sarees, preserving this rich cultural heritage while offering contemporary designs.
